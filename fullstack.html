About this course

Our Full Stack Software development course is an immersive experience that takes you from beginner to junior software developer in 24 weeks. At the end of the course, you will know how to build world-class and Human-centered softwares, understand web-application workflow and work across cross-functional teams of developers, designers and product managers.

What You'll Learn

Our full stack software development course follows a step by step approach to helping you learn irrespective of your prior knowledge of tech, until you can build your own web applications from scratch.

Intro to Web Development – PART 1

Our Fullstack Course is very intense. And starting this way will help you prepare for the next 6 Months for which you will be in this program. This section of the program will require you put in a minimum of 24hrs of classwork room time a week and 10hours of personal practice and a total of 48 hours for the next 2 Weeks to cover the curriculum

At the end of this 2 Weeks you will learn;


  • Web 101, how the web works
  • Terminal basic commands
  • Git basic commands
  • HTML / CSS foundations
  • JavaScript foundations
  • Tech workflows and tools in a startup

UI/UX Design – PART 2

In this program, students will learn design thinking, ideation, wireframing, prototyping and how to build their first minimum viable product. Here students will master how to create a world-class User interface and user experience for every product they will be building in the future.
